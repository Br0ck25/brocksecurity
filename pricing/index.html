What Affects Pricing

Understanding Your Investment

Several factors influence the final cost of your security camera system

Number of Cameras

More cameras mean broader coverage and higher costs. We help you find the optimal number to cover all critical areas without overspending.

Camera Quality

Higher resolution cameras (4K vs 1080p) provide clearer images but cost more. We'll recommend the right resolution based on your monitoring needs.

Storage Capacity

Larger hard drives store more footage. We calculate the right storage based on how many cameras you have and how long you want to keep recordings.

Installation Complexity

Property size, wall types, cable routing, and mounting locations affect installation time and cost. We provide accurate estimates after a site visit.

Additional Features

Advanced features like AI detection, two-way audio, PTZ cameras, or smart home integration add to the total investment but provide enhanced functionality.

Warranty & Support

All our systems include lifetime support at no extra cost. We're always available to help troubleshoot, adjust settings, or answer questions about your system.
