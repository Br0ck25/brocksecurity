Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 4, 2025

Brock Security ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Data

Personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and property address, that you voluntarily provide when requesting quotes or contacting us.

Derivative Data

Information our servers automatically collect when you access the Site, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and access times.

How We Use Your Information

SMS and Phone Communications

By providing your phone number and giving consent on our forms, you agree to receive text messages and phone calls from us regarding appointments, quotes, and offers.

Opt-Out

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time.
